Logo Legends

From a Paintbrush to Mouse: Master the Art of Design!

Rules

  • The event will have two rounds.
  • Each team will consist of three students.
  • A maximum of two teams can participate from each school.

Round 1:

  • A topic will be provided on the day of the competition to design logos.
  • Each team will have 30 minutes to create a logo based on the given topic.
  • Logos must be made on paper using any medium (teams must bring their own materials).
  • Each logo will be scored individually according to specific rubrics.
  • The top five teams will advance to the next round.

Round 2:

  • The second round will be digital, using the ____ _____ app (likely a free Adobe app).
  • Instructions for using the app will be shared in advance.
  • The second round will take place on school computers, where a desktop with a keyboard, mouse, and the app pre-installed will be provided.
  • Graphic tablets and styluses are not allowed.
  • Teams may use images from the internet, but the final logo must not be overly similar to existing ones.
  • AI tools are strictly prohibited.
