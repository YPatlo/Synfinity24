Vortex Ventures

Design, Innovate, Present: Fashion Meets Function!

Rules

  1. Each team will consist of 4 members.
  2. Teams must design a fashion accessory that is also practical for daily use (e.g., a purse with a cup holder).
  3. Teams will present their product to a panel of judges.
  4. Teams may use presentations, posters, etc., during their pitch.
  5. Any presentations must be emailed to our address by 3:00 p.m. on 21st November, 2024.
  6. A functioning product is required; images or virtual recreations will not be accepted.
  7. Teams will have 30 minutes to prepare for their presentations on the day of the event.
