Gnan Srishti

Gnan Srishti School of Excellence is a renowned school located in the heart of HSR Layout, Bangalore. It is affiliated to the CISCE for the ICSE and ISC and has produced marvelous results consistently. Established in 2009, Gnan Srishti’s mission has always been to provide Balanced Value Based Education. To celebrate the completion of 15 years in nurturing young minds, GSSE has proposed to host a student led inter-school event.

What is Synfinity?

Gnan Srishti, presents its first ever interschool extravaganza for students from Grade IX - XII, Synfinity, a premier platform crafted to unveil the multifaceted talents of students from literature to art to science. This exceptional event promises a series of unique, challenging, and exhilarating competitions designed to nurture healthy competition, cultivate teamwork, and promote sportsmanship. Synfinity consists of 7 events. Mark your calendars for the 22nd of November and get ready to witness history in the making! Be prepared for the fun, thrill and challenge that comes your way as you compete with students from numerous schools and battle it out for the prizes!
